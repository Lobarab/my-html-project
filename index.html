To visit Uzbekistan is to find oneself to be immersed in experiencing the bygone times of once the most important trade route The Great Silk Road and the ancient cities that connected East and West.

Uzbekistan is a mysterious place that captivates you with its splendour cities and their ancient magnificient buildings showcasing the intricate architecture and turquoise coloured domes. Its nature with sharp contrasts of mountains, deserts and green lush valleys will enchant any traveller just like it did Marco Polo. You will find yourself lost in Bukhara's old town exploring its majestic monuments and become awe-struck by the beauty of Samarkand's Registan. Bazars of each city will welcome you with its colourful stalls of spices, sweets and silk.

We are delighted offer tailored tours to visit the ancient cities of Uzbekistan, unique for each and every explorer whether you would like to have a solo trip or join a group of like minded people.
